If you are a student or a professional

Welcome

To make precise and beautiful Resumes, you need to login using Google Sign-in. Click on "Login with Google" at the top-right corner of the page.

Select the template

We have several beautiful templates available, which help you create concise and readable CVs.

Make your Resume

Fill in your personal details, educational qualifications, skills, hobbies, etc. in the resume template.

Download

After completing the details, click on the Print/Save button to download your resume as a PDF.

If you are a Placement Cell of an institute

Sign In

As a placement cell representative, you can upload templates your institute uses. Sign in or register to get started.

Register your Institute

Enter the details in the form and click register. We will verify and contact you within 2–3 days.

Upload the template

Once logged in, click on upload to add your institute's resume PDF template.

Wait

After uploading the template, we will activate it for your students within 2–3 days.

For further queries, email us at admin@cvmaker.com
